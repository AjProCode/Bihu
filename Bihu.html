Bihu

A wonderful, fun and intresting festival celebrated all across the globe

Why is it celebrated?

Each Bihu coincides with an idiosyncratic phase in the farming calendar. The Bohag Bihu marks the New Year at the advent of seeding time, the Kati Bihu marks the c ompletion of sowing and transplanting of paddies, and the Magh Bihu marks the end of the harvesting period.
